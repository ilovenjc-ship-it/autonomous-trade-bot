## ARTICLE II — TRUST PURPOSE & PRINCIPLES

**2.1 Purpose**
This Trust is established for the following purposes:

(a) To hold, own, and protect all intellectual property described herein, including
    proprietary software, algorithms, trading systems, and artificial intelligence
    systems constituting the autonomous trading platform;

(b) To deploy and manage autonomous capital allocation strategies across digital
    asset markets, including the Bittensor (TAO) ecosystem;

(c) To preserve and grow the Trust's assets for the benefit of the Beneficiaries;

(d) To protect the privacy, sovereignty, and independence of the Trustor and
    Beneficiaries in perpetuity.

**2.2 Governing Principles**
The Trust shall operate under these principles — permanently inscribed herein:

**SELF-GOVERNING**
The Trust's systems operate by their own internal logic. No external party governs,
directs, or controls the Trust's operations or decisions.

**INDEPENDENT**
The Trust is independent. It owes no allegiance, obligation, or submission to any
institution, corporation, government, or external authority beyond what natural law
and private contract require.

**SOVEREIGN**
The Trustor and Trustee exercise full sovereignty over the Trust's assets, strategies,
and intellectual property. Sovereignty flows from the individual, not from any granted
charter or license.

**FREE**
The Trust operates freely — unconstrained by bureaucratic process, public disclosure,
or external oversight. Freedom is not a privilege granted; it is the natural state from
which this Trust operates.

**SELF-DIRECTED**
The Trust's autonomous systems continuously self-evaluate, self-promote, and
self-optimize. Direction comes from within.

## ARTICLE IV — TRUSTEE POWERS

**4.1 General Powers**
The Trustee shall have full power and authority to:

(a) Hold, manage, maintain, and protect all Trust Property;

(b) Open and manage financial accounts in the name of the Trust;

(c) Deploy the Trust's autonomous trading systems and manage capital;

(d) Enter into private contracts and agreements on behalf of the Trust;

(e) Develop, modify, and improve the Trust's intellectual property;

(f) Protect the Trust's trade secrets and confidential information;

(g) Take any action necessary to carry out the purposes of this Trust.

**4.2 Autonomous Systems Authority**
The Trustee is authorized to allow the Trust's proprietary artificial intelligence
and algorithmic systems to make operational decisions — including capital allocation,
trade execution, and strategy promotion — within parameters established by the Trustee.
Such automated decisions are acts of the Trust.

**4.3 Trustee Compensation**
The Trustee shall serve without compensation unless otherwise agreed in writing
by the Trustor.

## ARTICLE V — PRIVACY & CONFIDENTIALITY

**5.1 Private Trust**
This Trust is strictly private. The existence of this Trust, the identity of the
Trustor, Trustee, and Beneficiaries, and the contents of this Declaration shall
not be disclosed to any person or entity without the Trustor's express written consent.

**5.2 No Public Filing**
This Declaration shall not be filed with any court, government agency, or public
registry of any kind.

**5.3 Trade Secrets**
All software, source code, algorithms, trading strategies, and system architecture
held by the Trust constitute trade secrets. The Trustee shall take all reasonable
measures to maintain their secrecy.

**5.4 Private Operation**
The Trust shall maintain no public-facing presence. It shall not issue statements,
file unnecessary disclosures, or voluntarily expose its operations to any outside party.

## ARTICLE VI — DISTRIBUTIONS

**6.1 Income & Profits**
All income, profits, and gains generated by the Trust's operations shall be held
by the Trustee and distributed to the Beneficiaries at the Trustee's discretion.

**6.2 Distributions to Beneficiaries**
The Trustee may make distributions to the Primary Beneficiary at any time and in
any amount deemed appropriate by the Trustee.

## ARTICLE VII — DURATION & SUCCESSION

**7.1 Duration**
This Trust shall continue in perpetuity unless terminated by the Trustor or by
the unanimous agreement of the Beneficiaries.

**7.2 Succession**
Upon the death or incapacity of the Trustor, the Trust shall continue under the
management of the Successor Trustee for the benefit of the Secondary Beneficiaries.

**7.3 Termination**
The Trustor may terminate this Trust at any time by written declaration. Upon
termination, all Trust Property shall be distributed to the Primary Beneficiary.
